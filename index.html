About Holi Festival

Holi is a popular ancient Hindu festival, also known as the "Festival of Love", the "Festival of Colors", and the "Festival of Spring". It celebrates the eternal and divine love of Radha and Krishna. The festival also signifies the triumph of good over evil.

During this vibrant celebration, people play with colors, water, have parties, and dance under water sprinklers. Special Holi delicacies include gujiya, malpuas, mathri, puran poli and more.
